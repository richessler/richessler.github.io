WHO I AM

Hi, I'm Richard - an analytically-minded engineer who approaches problems both quantitatively and qualitatively. My professional ambitions and aspirations are fueled by my passion and obsession for web development and everything tech-related. With that said, I do not believe I would have made it half as far as I have today without my sense of humor or ability to laugh at myself from time-to-time. My goal as a developer is to join forces with a shop whose ideals resonate with my personal interests.I want to put my talents to good use and add value to a cohesive team.

Formally a Civil Engineer, I have now turned full-stack web developer thanks to the Web Development Immersive course from General Assembly in Washington, D.C. This course has rekindled my love for programming and inspired me to learn anything and everything there is to learn about the industry. When I'm not working through a coding problem or attending local tech meetups, you can usually find me cooking, biking, making wine, or studying for my next level of Sommelier certification.
